About me,,

I am an artist and teacher with years of experience in drawing and teaching to both beginners and professionals in the fields of fine arts and architecture. This has given me the ability to be creative, discover the flaws in art, and harness the secrets of its beauty. I use these skills in conjunction with continuous learning and daily visual nourishment to present art in all its forms to society through an educational art organization I established in 2017. My deep understanding of the beauty and randomness of nature through my professional photography work has strengthened my desire to constantly explore this creativity and create a parallel to it. I am currently looking for a position where I can gain more experience and add it to my previous expertise to develop, disseminate, and teach art more widely..
